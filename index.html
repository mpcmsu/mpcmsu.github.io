About MPC

Who We Are

The Marketing Psychology Club (MPC) is a student-led organization in the Faculty of Business Management and Professional Studies (FBMP), dedicated to exploring how psychological principles can be applied to create more effective marketing strategies.

We bring together students from various courses who share an interest in understanding consumer behavior, decision-making processes, and the psychological triggers that influence purchasing decisions.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between psychological theory and marketing practice through research, discussion, and real-world applications.

Our Vision

Create a community of forward-thinking marketers who understand the psychology behind consumer decisions.

What We Do

Workshops, case studies, guest lectures, and hands-on projects to apply psychological principles to marketing.

Benefits

Networking, skill development, and practical experience that enhances your marketing career.
